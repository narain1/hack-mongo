## Day-by-Day Itinerary

### Day 1: Eiffel Tower & Louvre Museum
Start your trip with a visit to the iconic Eiffel Tower. Next, explore the world-renowned Louvre Museum.

**Lunch Recommendation:** Le Jules Verne (Eiffel Tower, 5 Avenue Gustave Eiffel, 75007 Paris, France)  

### Day 2: Notre Dame Cathedral & Seine River Cruise
Visit the historic Notre Dame Cathedral. In the evening, enjoy a romantic Seine River Cruise.

**Dinner Recommendation:** Les Deux Magots (6 Place Saint-Germain des Prés, 75006 Paris, France)  

### Day 3: Versailles Palace
Spend your final day touring the grand Versailles Palace.

**Lunch Recommendation:** Angelina (Versailles, First Floor of the Pavillon d'Orléans, 78000 Versailles, France)
